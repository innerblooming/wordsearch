# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026/06/09

## Privacy Policy for Word Search

Thank you for playing **Word Search** ("the Game").

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Game on iOS and Android devices.

## Information We Collect

The Game does **not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information** from users.

We do not:

- Require user registration or accounts
- Collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- Collect location information
- Use analytics services
- Display third-party advertisements
- Track users across apps or websites

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device unless your device's operating system provides its own backup or synchronization services.

## Third-Party Services

The Game does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising, or marketing services.

However, the Game is distributed through platform providers such as Apple and Google. These providers may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies and terms of service.

## Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults.

Because no personal information is collected, there is no personal information to disclose, share, or sell.

## Data Security

Since the Game does not collect or store personal information on our servers, we do not maintain user databases containing personal information.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by changing the "Last Updated" date above.
